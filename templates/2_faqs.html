Hows does the voting work?

This online voting system uses technology "blockchain" to record your vote such that is cannot be changed.

What is blockchain?

Blockchain is a way of storing data in a chain that is linked together through a unique id. When data is stored, it is broadcast to many places storing data using what is known as a peer to peer network. This means many people are able to see and view the data stored when your vote is collected.

How can I know my vote will be counted correctly?

This tool provides a self verification system whereby you can check your vote for a short period of time after voting.
